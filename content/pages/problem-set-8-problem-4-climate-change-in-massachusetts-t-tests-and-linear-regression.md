In this problem, students use R to model the average annual temperature in Fahrenheit in Massachusetts for each year from 1895 to 2023, and to determine if the temperature has significantly increased. The data is in climate-data-MA.csv, which is sourced from {{% resource_link "05cf152b-6aba-4806-ae47-016870651943" "NOAA's Statewide Time Series" %}}.
